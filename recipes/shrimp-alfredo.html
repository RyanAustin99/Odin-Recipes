Shrimp Alfredo

A beautiful spin on a classic recipe, passed down by my father.

Get lost in a sea of flavors as you dive into this creamy ocean of alfredo sauce and seared shrimp.

Theres really no pasta sauce quite like the famous alfredo, but what if we added some cream cheese? What if we changed the noodles?

What if we completely changed the game with...CHICKEN BROTH. Thats right folks. get those swollen bellies ready for the time of their lives.

This one is going to change your Life!

Ingredients List:

  • shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 ounces fettucine pasta
  • 2 tablestoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 4 ounces chream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • 2 cups heavy cream

  1. Cook pasta according to package until al dente. Drain, rinse, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrent.
  3. Add shrimp to skillet and cook 2-3 minutes on each side or until they are pink and cureld. Add salt, remove from skillet, and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, pour the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer. Allow it to cook for a few minutes to redeuce slightly.
  5. Reduce the heat and add the cream cheese and heavy cream. Stir continuously until well combined.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat it evenly with the creamy sauce.
  7. Stir in the grated parmesan cheese until its melted and the sauce is smooth
  8. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, gently folding them into the pasta and sauce mixture.
  9. Once everything has heated through, remove the skillet from the heat.
  10. Served the seared shrimp alfredo hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

Dive into decadence: Savoutr this seared shrimp alfredo, where creamy meets comfort.
