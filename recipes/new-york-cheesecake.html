New York Cheesecake

This take on a New York Cheesecake will have your friends amazed!

If you dont have friends, I promise, this is the cheesecake to drown those sorrows in.

Creamy and rich, this cheesecake features a velvety cream cheese filling that's perfectly complimented by the sweet and tangy strawberries.

You will NEVER go back to regular cheesecake, again.

Ingredients (crust)

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs.
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar.
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter.

Ingredients (Cheesecake Filling)

  • 4 8-ounce packages of softened cream cheese.
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar.
  • 4 large eggs.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, diced into small bits
  • Sliced strawberries for garnish (Topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325f or 160c
  2. In a medium bowl, combine cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter
  3. Press mixture evenly along the bottom of the pan to form the crust. Use the back of a spoon to press firmly.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  6. Fold in the sour cream until fully incorporated into the batter.
  7. Gently fold in the diced strawberries until evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  8. Pour the cheesecake fillinf over the prepared crust in the springform pan.
  9. Once the salmon is cooked, carefully remove the skillet from the oven.
  10. Tap the pan gently on the counter to release any air bubbles.
  11. Place the pan on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  12. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door closed for an additional hour to cool gradually.
  13. After cooling, remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature.
  14. Once cooled, refridgerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours
  15. Before serving, garnish the cheesecake with fresh strawberry bits and enjoy!

This decadent dessert is sure to impress with its creamy texture and bursts of strawberry flavor in every bite!
